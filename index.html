About Me

I am postdoctoral associate at the MIT Laboratory for Information & Decision Systems, where I am hosted by Ali Jadbabie , Devavrat Shah , and Suvrit Sra . Previously, I completed my PhD studies under the supervision of Michael I. Jordan and Benjamin Recht in the Department of Computer Science at University of California, Berkeley, and I received a bachelor's degree in Mathematics from Princeton University, where I was advised by Sébastien Bubeck.

I am broadly interested in the foundations of machine learning, with an inclination towards reinforcement learning and control theory.
